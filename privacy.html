Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Who we are

Entheos ("we", "our") is a business management app for self-employed service providers. The app is operated out of Canada and this policy applies to the Entheos mobile and desktop apps and the entheosapp.ca website.

2. Information we collect

Entheos is offline-first. Most of your data stays on your device unless you choose to create an account and sync to the cloud.

Data you enter into the app

  • Business information (name, owner, province, industry, tax details)
  • Client records (names, contact details, notes, custom fields)
  • Appointments, services, products, expenses, payments, income
  • Receipt photos, mileage logs, and other records you add

Account data (optional)

  • Email address and password (for sign-in)
  • The data above, copied to our server so you can sync across devices

Phone contacts (only if you import them)

If you use the "Import from Phone Contacts" feature, Entheos reads your device's contact list so you can select which ones to add as clients. We do not upload your full contact list anywhere — only the contacts you explicitly select become clients in your account.

3. How we use your data

  • To provide the core app features (scheduling, record-keeping, tax tools)
  • To sync your data between your devices, if you have an account
  • To send you appointment reminder notifications on your own device
  • To secure and back up your account data

We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Where your data is stored

When you use Entheos without an account, all your data lives on your device. If you create an account, your data is also encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and stored on our Supabase-based backend hosted in Canada. Row-level security ensures only you can read your own records. Receipt photos are stored in a private cloud bucket with per-user access controls.

5. Your rights

You can:

  • Access, export, or delete any of your data from within the app
  • Delete your account at any time (this removes your data from our servers)
  • Use the app entirely offline without ever creating an account
  • Request a copy of your data or its deletion by emailing us

6. Permissions we request

  • Camera / Photos: to capture or choose receipt images (only when you tap the camera button)
  • Contacts: to import phone contacts as clients (only when you use that feature)
  • Notifications: to remind you of upcoming appointments

Each permission is only used for its stated purpose and can be revoked from your device settings.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted transit (HTTPS/TLS), database-level row security, hashed passwords, and signed URLs for any uploaded files. No system is perfectly secure; if we ever discover a breach affecting your data, we will notify you promptly.

8. Children's privacy

Entheos is designed for self-employed business owners and is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 16.

9. Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, notify you through the app.

10. Contact

Questions, data requests, or concerns? Email privacy@entheosapp.ca.
